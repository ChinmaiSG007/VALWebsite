Welcome to VAL, your trusted partner in civil management and consulting. We specialize in providing top-tier services to ensure the success and quality of your projects.

NDT for Civil Structures

Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) for civil structures is a critical service we offer to assess the integrity and safety of existing structures without causing any damage. Our NDT services include:
  • Ultrasonic Testing: Using high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws and measure material thickness.
  • Radiographic Testing: Employing X-rays or gamma rays to create images of the internal structure of components.
  • Magnetic Particle Testing: Identifying surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials.
  • Dye Penetrant Testing: Detecting surface-breaking defects in non-porous materials.
